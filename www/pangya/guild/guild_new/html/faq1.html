1. Club FAQ
1. What is an official club?
Official club is a community system, so players can have a closed environment to talk to and battle other clubs in the game.
2. What are the benefits of joining a club?
The player will be able to write on the club bulletin board, participate in a closed community, in game will have the club mark will be shown before nickname, chat in the club and be able to battle with other clubs.
3. What will happen to the club members?
Will be part of a closed community created by another player the "Club Master":
  ・ Chat in the club chat within the game;
  ・ Club mark will be shown before nickname and in tournaments;
  ・ And create or participate in club battles.
