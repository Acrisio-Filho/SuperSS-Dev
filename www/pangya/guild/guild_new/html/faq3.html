3. Club creation and management CASE STUDY
1. What is “Club Master”?
Club Master is the person who created and manages the club.
2. Cannot create a club with the same club name.
Each club has its own name, this is its identifier so it cannot have more than one club with the same name.
3. I created a club. When is official approval?
When the GM checks all club registration data, if it does not meet the terms of use, the GM approves the club.
4. How long is the “Preliminary Club” period?
The GM verification process may take from 1 to 7 days from the club registration date.
5. Club creation is not possible.
You are not a Rookie E or larger to create a club,
or GM has blocked your account from being able to create a club.
In the latter case contact one of the GM for the reason.
6. I would like to close the club.
Only the club master who can close him.
have to expel all other club members except you.
Enter your club page by clicking on the "My Club Page" button,
After that click on the "Club Info Change" button to enter the club setup page,
then click on the "Club closed" link and then read the conditions and then click on the "Execute" button.
The club will be in the process of closing for 7 days, before completing the 7 days you can cancel the club closure.
After the 7 days of the club closure process has passed it will be deleted and you are free to create or join another club.
7. When does a “club blocked” occur? What are the measures and solutions in that case?
Your club has been blocked by GM, contact one of the GM to find out why.
